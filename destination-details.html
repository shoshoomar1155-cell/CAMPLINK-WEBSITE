Bali – Beach & Adventure Camp

Trip name: Bali Student Beach Camp • Type: Beach + Light Hiking • Duration: 5 days / 4 nights

Bali offers warm beaches, green rice fields, and a relaxed student atmosphere. This trip is a mix between fun on the beach and short guided hikes to waterfalls.

Included activities

  • Welcome campfire night on the beach with student games.
  • Short hiking trail to a nearby waterfall with a guide.
  • Sunrise yoga session by the sea.
  • Visit to a traditional village to learn about Balinese culture.

Approximate price: $280 per student (including accommodation and local transport).

Cappadocia, Turkey – Camping & Hot Air Balloons

Trip name: Cappadocia Student Camp • Type: Camping & Light Adventure • Duration: 4 days / 3 nights

Cappadocia is known for its unique rock formations and colorful hot air balloons. This trip focuses on camping, star watching, and exploring valleys safely with guides.

Included activities

  • Two nights of student camping in a supervised campsite.
  • Guided walk through one of the famous valleys.
  • Optional hot air balloon flight at sunrise (extra fee).
  • Evening gathering with Turkish snacks and cultural stories.

Approximate price: $250 per student (camping gear included).
